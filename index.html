About

Cantilever is not just another tech conference — it’s a celebration of coding as a form of art. Over three inspiring days, Cantilever brings together forward-thinking developers, designers, and innovators to explore how technology can be more than lines of code: it can be storytelling, performance, and creative expression.

From keynote talks to immersive workshops, Cantilever offers a unique space where creativity meets technology. Whether you’re building your first interactive project or experimenting with generative art and AI, you’ll find ideas, mentors, and collaborators here. Our goal is to empower you with both the technical skills and the artistic mindset to see coding in a whole new light.

Why Attend

  • Learn from visionaries: Hear from speakers who are redefining the boundaries of coding, design, and digital art.
  • Hands-on experience: Participate in practical workshops where you’ll create projects that combine technology with imagination.
  • Be inspired: Watch live coding performances and demos that turn code into music, visuals, and interactive experiences.
  • Collaborate and showcase: Team up with fellow attendees to solve creative challenges and present your work to the community.
  • Expand your network: Connect with developers, artists, startups, and mentors who share your passion for innovation.

FAQ

Where is the event?

Cantilever 2025 will be hosted in New Delhi, India. The venue is a modern conference center equipped with collaborative spaces, auditoriums, and breakout rooms designed to foster creativity. Full details, including directions and accommodation options, will be provided upon registration.

Do I need prior experience?

Not at all. Cantilever is designed for curious minds of all levels. Beginners will find accessible workshops and supportive mentors, while experienced developers can dive into advanced sessions on generative art, AI, and cutting-edge creative tools.

Will sessions be recorded?

Yes! All keynote talks and most workshops will be recorded and made available exclusively to registered participants. You’ll be able to revisit sessions, catch up on anything you missed, and keep learning even after the event ends.

How can I connect with other attendees?

Networking is at the heart of Cantilever. You’ll have access to community meetups, collaborative hack sessions, and online discussion groups before, during, and after the conference. Many of our past attendees have gone on to launch projects and startups together!
